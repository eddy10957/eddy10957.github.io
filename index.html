About Me

Hello, I'm Edoardo Troianiello, an iOS developer based in Piano di Sorrento, Naples, with over two years of experience in the iOS domain and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering.
My passion for programming has led me to explore a wide range of languages, including C, C++, Java, Swift, SQL, and Python.

However, my primary focus and expertise now lie in Swift, especially SwiftUI. I've honed my skills by working with various iOS frameworks, such as CoreData, Vapor, CoreML, AVFoundation, SceneKit, and SpriteKit. Additionally, I've incorporated external packages like AudioKit and SwiftUI-Charts into my projects.

One of the most significant milestones in my career was during my second year at the Apple Developer Academy, where I was selected among the top 60 students. During the second year at the Apple Developer Academy, I had the opportunity to immerse myself in a real work environment. This invaluable experience allowed me to work on practical projects, collaborate in agile teams, and interface with stakeholders.
